My Gods and Goddesses


The Higher Being/Power

A formless being with greater power than all other gods. Not a god, just a greater force. Can destroy all gods and everything with the snap of a finger if they wished. They can bring them back with the same ease.

????

God of the Unknown. Created by the higher being. The higher being believed that no one should know everything and then created ????. He is the unknown, the unseen, what lurks in the shadows, what you want to know but can never know. No one but the higher being knows what he is, what he truly looks like, or what his name is. Depicted as being the partner of the higher being. Created death (Dara) because he was told to because Yagi made humans.

Yagi

A goddess born from the higher being’s right ring finger. Protects earth. Had children to rule Earth for her. Her four children are Ari, Volca, Tia, Serena. She created two other children, twins, Ganandal and Grandali; these two will always have an adolescent form. Yagi created two more children, both were created fully-formed and both were made after she sculpted the first humans.

Yami

A god born from the higher being’s left ring finger. Protects and rules the Sun and moon.

Ari

Air goddess, goddess of air/wind/clouds. The goddess with the most power over earth and the other gods. She originally hated earth and hated being there but after her sister got banished to the innerworld, she and her three sisters got parts of the earth to rule. Ari got the sky, because the kingdom of the gods is in the sky she now has rule over most of the gods.

Volca

Fire goddess, goddess of fire, lava, heat, and other things relating to that. A goddess that was punished to live in the center of the earth and rule the Innerworld(Underworld) forever. She can never leave. The only other god she usually ever sees is he has two children, identical twin sons, Menunu and Mozuzu. Married to Dara, no one else knows about this though because Dara doesn’t speak and she can’t leave the Innerworld.

Tia

Earth goddess, goddess of earth, plants, dirt, and other earthy things. Though she is the earth goddess she doesn’t have the most power, but she does have the most power over what goes on on land. Earth looks the way it does because of her.

Serena

Water goddess, goddess of water. She has rule over water, bodies of water, and everything that goes on in the water. A sweet and gentle goddess. She is quite literally loved by all. Followers of Volca and followers of Serena dislike each other.

Ganandal

Yagi’s only son, twin brother of Grandali. He is supposed to represent good in the world and that good can't exist without evil.

Grandali

Yagi’s daughter, twin sister of Ganandal. She is supposed to represent evil in the world and that evil can’t exist without good.

Dara

Created by ???? because the higher being said to, the higher being told him to do so as a result of Yagi making humans. Genderless god of death. Brings the dead to the innerworld. Never speaks, is said to be kind to and help where they can. Very mysterious, doesn’t show face, wears hood and cloak, carries scythe. Some stories have them as being a parent of Menunu and Mozuzu, though truly he isn’t, just a step father. Has a daughter, Cynza, she was born when Dara themself with their scythe, only to be reborn again. Married to Volca, no one else knows about this though because Dara doesn’t speak and Volca can’t leave the Innerworld.

Sara

“The Fairy Goddess”. Mother of all fairies, magic, and monsters. She has wings unlike all other gods, with the exception of Sol and Sal. Only two of her children are gods, Sol and Sal. She was born when the first flower wilted and the first petal to fall touched the ground. Married to Charutoph.

Sal

The goddess and incarnation of ice. A trickster goddess that isn’t hesitant to show her dislike to others. Rules with her younger brother, Sol. The fatherless daughter of Sara. She feels as though Tia was just pitying them when she gave the land, but still accepted it, though she still has Tia on her hate list.

Sol

The god and incarnation of snow. A calm, yet rude, cold, and cruel god. Rules the cold part of earth with his older sister, Sal. The fatherless son of Sara. Doesn’t hate Tia.

Charutoph

God of love, sex, beauty, and fertility. A dumb god, can’t read a room. Highly inappropriate. Despite this, many can’t help but love him. Loved by all of the other gods, despite them sometimes finding him annoying. Was not born, but more created by Yagi fully formed. Married to Sara.

Phospho

A god that always keeps his face hidden by a mask. Much like Dara, he is quiet and mysterious. He is often rude so no reason other than he can. God of lies, distrust, disrespect, and other things of the like. Many other gods dislike him as he is very cocky and arrogant. Not born, but created by Grandali fully formed.

Mozuzu

Twin brother of Menunu, son of Volca (Sometimes Volca and Dara). The brothers together represent peace and war. Mozuzu is war.

Menunu

Twin brother of Mozuzu, son of Volca (Sometimes Volca and Dara). The brothers together represent peace and war. Menunu is peace. Menunu has a daughter, Kismet.

Kismet

Fate, destiny. She decides the fate of every being on earth that are not gods. When a god wants something to happen to something they don’t control, they go to her. She has the most rule over the humans. Daughter of Menunu. Born from Menunu‘s eye when it was pulled out by Mozuzu. Married to Cynza.

Cynza

The Goddess of new comings, new beginnings, reincarnation. The youngest of them all. Beloved by all, except Sol and Sal of course. Born when Dara killed themself with their scythe. Married to Kismet.
